The Rocket Launcher (a.k.a. Stomp Rocket) is a toy of which you push on the cap with a great amount of force and it sends the rocket flying! It took close to 8 hours to print and mere minutes to assemble. It works by creating an air-tight cylinder, that when compressed at a high speed, forces the rocket up and out.
