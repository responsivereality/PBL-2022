The Disc Launcher is akin to the BeyBlade, because it is essentially a spinning top fired by spinning the crank. It was 100% 3D printed, taking a total of 12 hours to print and 30 minutes to assemble.

The launcher works by using a 13.5 to 1 gear ratio, meaning that every time the crank rotates once the blade rotates 13.5 times. This allows for exceptionally fast speeds to be met with minimal effort.
