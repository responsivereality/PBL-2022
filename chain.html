  • Chainmail

Chainmail is a cloth-like 3D print that uses interlocking rectangles or rings to create a flexible body. It took around an hour to print, and is much like the chainmail armour from popular video games and movies.

3D printing is one of the only and easiest ways to make chainmail, as with traditional manufacturing, it would be impossible to have the rings interlocking without a gap. 3D printing can do this as it produces one layer at a time, meaning that they are produced interlocking.
